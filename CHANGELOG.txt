## [4.0.0]
Additions:
	- Added support for lots of objects.
Changes:
	- The addon no longer alerts the player when an item is updated.
	- The default date format is now DAY/MONTH/YEAR. This can't be changed.
Removals:
	- Quest rewards are no longer tracked.
	- Loot from the mailbox is no longer tracked. (This specifically affects items acquired from the Auction House.)
	- Items purchased from merchants are no longer tracked.
